About

Justin Rhinesmith is co-founder and CTO of MoveMeFit. Previously he worked in gaming at Kabam as a senior engineering manager - primarily on Edgeworld and Dragons of Atlantis (the latter grossing over $100 million during its lifetime). Before Kabam, Justin built and launched several applications on multiple platforms for BDMetrics (later Informous) in Columbia, MD. He holds bachelors degrees in Computer Science and Mathematical Sciences from Ball State University.
